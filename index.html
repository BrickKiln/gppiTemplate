Hallo!

I made a GPPi theme for this really popular presentation package. It's not the easiest to use, nor the most convenient, but it's snappy and powerful. Let me show you what this is capable of.

It just works!

The slides can be hosted online and viewed on browser. That means it works on all devices and platforms. No need to fumble with thumbdrives and emails, or worry about missing fonts anymore!

Okay I lied. It might not work really well on older versions of Internet Explorer. But you should not use older versions of Internet Explorer anyway.

Vertical Slides

You can nest slides inside each other. Nested slides move vertically.

Arrows in the bottom right corner let you know which direction you should move for the next slide.

Nested slides are useful for adding additional detail underneath a high level horizontal slide.
